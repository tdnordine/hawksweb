A Short History

Veterans Athletic Complex consists of four lighted adult softball fields with 285’ foot fences, four full-size multi-purpose fields (one has lights) and one baseball field with a 385’ foot fence. The complex also includes a playground set and two buildings with indoor bathrooms and concessions.

Veterans Athletic Complex is operated by the City of Hastings Parks and Recreation Department and is open from April to October. Fields are used on a weekly basis by youth soccer, youth softball, adult softball, adult kickball, youth baseball, and the Hastings Hawks adult amateur baseball team. Fields can also be reserved by groups or organizations for practice, games, tournaments, or other functions.
