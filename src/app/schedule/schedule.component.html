Tickets

Tickets are $3 and are available at many local businesses who support the Hastings Hawks, as well as at the concessions stand at Vets Baseball Field 45 min. prior to game start.

Season Pass

Season Passes are also available for $40 and are good for all league and non-league home games except the playoffs.
