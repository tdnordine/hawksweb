Schedule and Ticket Information

Tickets

Tickets are $3 at the park, available at concessions 45 min prior to the game start or you can pay when one of our team members walks through to collect.

Throughout the season, we bring tickets to many of our sponsors to give to their customers/visitors for free. Stop in, thank them for supporting Hawks Baseball, shop, then come to one of our games for free!

Season Pass

Season Passes are also available at concessions for $40 and are good for all league and non-league home games except the playoffs.
