Joining the Hawks

Thank you for your interest in Joining the Hastings Hawks. Before you complete this form, please confirm you are eligible to play for the Hawks based on the rules established by the Minnesota Baseball Association (MBA). A copy of the eligibility rule can be found in the MBA Handbook, Article 9. The handbook can be found here

.

The Hawks firmly believe in players fulfilling their commitments to their High School, Legion or VFW teams. If you are still playing for any of those teams, we believe they take priority. The ability to play at that level is a limited time opportunity. Our agreement with the coaches of those teams does allow you to join us for practices, warm ups and play in games as long as it doesn't conflict with your primary team. If you have a scheduled game with the other team, you are expected to be there.
